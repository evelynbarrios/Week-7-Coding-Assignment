FIRE stands for Financial Independence Retire Early. This is done through an aggressive investing plan by finding ways to increase income and decrease expenses. Once you reach assets 25x your annual spending you are officiaally in a position to retire.



Find out your Retirement Number

Financial Indpependence Retiring Early is not about an age but rather a number you reach that will be enough to cover your annual costs. Most of the time people will invest in low cost index funds to reach this number quicker due to compound intererest. The follwoing is a table that will help you determine what your retirement number is.
